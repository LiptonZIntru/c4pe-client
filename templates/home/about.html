Development of Yummy Places

Yummy place were created due to a European Union project called Concepts for professional education in border regions (ATCZ23-C4PE).

Program: INTERREG V-A AT- CZ 2014 – 2020

Project duration: 07/2018 - 06/2021

Lead partner: Donau - Universität Krems

The project aims to establish cross-border professional education in Austria and The Czech republic.

In project were participated SŠIPF Brno and HTL Krems
